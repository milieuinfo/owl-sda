## Task: Generate instances for InstallationPropertyShape

**Target Shape:** `:InstallationPropertyShape`
**Target Class:** `:InstallationProperty`
**Goal:** CREATE at least 2 instances of `:InstallationProperty` — none exist yet.

### Context

Existing installations to link to:
- `ex:installation/furnace`
- `ex:installation/glass-former`

Also add back-references on the installations:

5. Add `ssn:hasProperty` back-references on the installation nodes.
