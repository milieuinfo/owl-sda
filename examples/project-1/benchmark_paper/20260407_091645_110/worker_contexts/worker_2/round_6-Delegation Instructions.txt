## Task: Generate instances for ObservationShape

**Target Shape:** `:ObservationShape`
**Target Class:** `:Observation`
**Goal:** CREATE at least 2 instances of `:Observation` — none exist yet.

### Context
MyGlassFactory takes measurements on emissions at its measurement points. Observations are SOSA-style measurement events linking a measurement procedure to an emission result.

Existing resources to reference:
- Emission points: `ex:emissionpoint/stack1`, `ex:emissionpoint/discharge1`
- Measurement points: `ex:measurementpoint/stack1`, `ex:measurementpoint/water-discharge`
- Emissions will be created by POOL-3 as: `ex:emission/co2-stack1`, `ex:emission/nox-stack1`

### Shape Constraints
| Property | Class | Cardinality | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| `sosa:hasFeatureOfInterest` | `:Emission` | exactly 1 (minCount 1, maxCount 1) | **REQUIRED** — link to the emission being measured |
| `sosa:hasResult` | `sosa:Result` | 0..1 | Optional — link to observation result |
